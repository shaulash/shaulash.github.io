Food Hubs

Foodbank

We endeavour to deliver a comprehensive food bank service to support those who struggle to provide for themselves and their families. Open to people from all backgrounds, races or religions. We work with local charities and suppliers to provide basic food needs. Our food bank is open 6 days a week, 4.30 pm – 5.30 pm and closed on Sundays.

32 Dixon Avenue, Glasgow, G42 8EJ

Al-Khair Foundation Glasgow

Al-Khair Glasgow is part of the Al-Khair Foundation which is is a UK-based international NGO that provides emergency relief and developmental support in some of the world’s most impoverished areas. Al-Khair Glasgow provides a variety of local community based services and support including a foodbank.
