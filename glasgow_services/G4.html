Food Hubs

Poverty Relief

Every Sunday, 1Pm - 3PM, our volunteers distribute food parcels to destitute asylum seekers and refugees. Every Ramadhan, we provide additional food packs to destitute asylum seekers. These food packs are a lifeline to our service users, some of whom have no income or accommodation. We aim to continue our food distribution and are working with our scholars to create a trust fund, through which funds can be given directly to impoverished and destitute people within Glasgow.
