Pollockshields Community Food Point

Affordable Food Boxes: £5 weekly packs containing fresh fruits, veggies, treats, milk voucher, local store voucher, tinned goods, and rice. Flexible Payments: Pay weekly by card or cash; receipts provided. Pay It Forward: Option to cover your own and/or someone else's subscription (£10 total). Halal Options: All food provided is halal. Community-Driven: Bulk buying and surplus donations ensure great value for all. Inclusive Environment: Open to all; enjoy tea and snacks with friendly chats. Multilingual Support: Volunteers available to assist with language needs. Referral System: Referrals accepted from organisations- email hello@pollokshieldsfoodpoint.org. Emergency Support: Immediate food assistance available with dignity and respect. Simple two day food bags available for people that are unable to pay. Suggested contribution: £1. This is emergency support and is not for ongoing use. Open: Sundays and Mondays, 3-5pm (no booking required)

423 Shields Road, Glasgow, G41 1NY

Glasgow South West Foodbank

“The foodbank was there when we really needed it, it was an absolute lifeline.” We don’t think anyone in our community should have to face going hungry. That’s why we provide three days’ nutritionally balanced emergency food and support to local people who are referred to us in crisis. We are part of a nationwide network of foodbanks, supported by The Trussell Trust, working to combat poverty and hunger across the UK.

Ibrox Foodbank, 65 Clifford Street, Glasgow, G51 1QH

Community Spaces

Our Community Spaces in Shawlands, Troon and Alloa offer social and canine companionship to people over 60. Everyone from the local community is welcome, and you will always be greeted with a friendly face. Come along and enjoy a free cuppa and a biscuit, engage in conversation with members from the local community, our staff and volunteers and enjoy the company of our companionship dogs, join in our free Group Dog Walks, and access our free pet food bank.

Glasgow , 988 Pollokshaws Road , Shawlands , G41 2HA
