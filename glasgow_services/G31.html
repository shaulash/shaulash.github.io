Food Hubs

The Everlasting Foodbank

The Everlasting Foodbank is a warm safe space for vulnerable and disadvantaged families and individuals. We provide support through warm meals on a pay as you can afford basis. People will also have access to toys and books for kids and once more established, they will use it as a hub for information whether that be for addiction, cost of living crisis support or general helpful information. Foodbank opening times 1st & 3rd Saturday (of every month) 1pm - 3pm. Every other Saturday 1pm - 2pm

12 Whitevale Street, Glasgow, G31 1QR

Glasgow North East Foodbank

"The foodbank was there when we really needed it, it was an absolute lifeline.” We don’t think anyone in our community should have to face going hungry. That’s why we provide three days’ nutritionally balanced emergency food and support to local people who are referred to us in crisis. We are part of a nationwide network of foodbanks, supported by The Trussell Trust, working to combat poverty and hunger across the UK.
